About Maro Services Hub

Maro Services Hub helps people in Nigeria find trusted local service providers such as electricians, plumbers, mechanics, cleaners, cooks, tailors, barbers, and more.

Service providers can list their businesses so customers can easily discover them, view important details, and contact them when they need help.

Listings go through payment and admin approval before they become public. This helps improve trust and safety for customers and providers using Maro Services Hub.
